Chart Features Guide

This chart includes interactive features that allow you to pan and zoom:

  • Pan: Click and drag anywhere on the chart to pan across the graph.
  • Zoom: Use the scroll wheel on your mouse to zoom in and out. You can also use pinch gestures on a touch-enabled device.
  • Zoom Reset: Double-click on the chart to reset the zoom level to the default view.

Note: The chart is designed to maintain the integrity of the data visualized regardless of the zoom level or panning position. Labels and values will scale accordingly to ensure readability and accuracy.

Bivariate Color Map: Each plot point on the graph is color-coded based on the changes in 'miles' and 'gas' data values relative to the previous year. For example, green indicates an increase in both miles and gas, red indicates a decrease in both, blue indicates an increase in miles but a decrease in gas, and orange indicates a decrease in miles but an increase in gas.
